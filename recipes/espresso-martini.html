Espresso Martini

Description

An espresso martini is a stylish and energizing cocktail that combines the rich, bold flavor of freshly brewed espresso with the smoothness of vodka and a touch of coffee liqueur, such as Kahlúa. Served in a chilled martini glass, this drink is typically garnished with a few coffee beans for a visually appealing touch. The result is a balanced and sophisticated cocktail that offers both the invigorating kick of caffeine and the warmth of alcohol, making it a popular choice for evening gatherings or as a post-dinner treat. The espresso martini perfectly blends the worlds of coffee and cocktails, offering a refined yet energetic drinking experience.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ fluid ounces vodka
  • 1 ½ fluid ounces coffee-flavored liqueur (such as Kahlua)
  • 1 fluid ounce white creme de cacao
  • 1 fluid ounce brewed espresso
  • 1 cup ice

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Pour vodka, coffee liqueur, crème de cacao, and espresso into a cocktail shaker; add ice, cover and shake until chilled.
  3. Strain into a chilled martini glass.
